When to Come In

Don't wait for it to get worse.

If any of these sound familiar, it's worth a conversation. Most patients don't need a referral to get started.

  • Pain that lingers beyond a few days
  • Stiffness or reduced range of motion
  • Numbness, tingling, or radiating pain
  • Recurring injuries from training or sport
  • Pain that disrupts sleep or daily life
  • Recovering from surgery or an accident
