Patient Resources

Frequently Asked Questions

Straightforward answers about treatment, insurance, and what to expect.

Do I need a referral?

No referral needed. New Jersey is a direct-access state, so you can schedule with our physical therapists and chiropractors directly. Some insurance plans may require a referral for reimbursement — our team will verify before your first visit.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes. We're in-network with most major insurance providers including Aetna, BCBS, Cigna, United Healthcare, and Medicare. We'll handle the verification and walk you through your benefits before treatment begins.

What should I expect during my first visit?

Plan for about 60 minutes. We start with a one-on-one evaluation — movement screening, postural assessment, and a deep conversation about your goals. You'll leave with a personalized care plan and usually begin treatment the same day.

How many visits will I need?

It varies by condition, but most patients see meaningful progress within 4–8 visits. We focus on long-term results, not endless appointments — your care plan will have a clear timeline and graduation criteria.

Do you treat sports injuries?

Absolutely. From weekend warriors to competitive athletes, we treat sprains, strains, tendinopathies, post-operative rehab, and return-to-sport programming. Our team works with athletes from local clubs, high schools, and Division I programs across Bergen County.
